Tutoring

Our tutoring program offers personalized support for students who need extra help. Experienced tutors are available to provide one-on-one assistance through video calls, helping students grasp difficult concepts and improve their skills.

Online Exams

We offer a variety of online assessments to help students evaluate their understanding of the material. These include:

  • Quizzes: Short assessments designed to test knowledge on specific topics.
  • Practice Tests: Comprehensive tests that simulate actual exam conditions.
  • Mock Exams: Full-length exams that provide valuable practice and feedback before the real thing.

These assessments are designed to simulate real testing conditions and provide immediate feedback, allowing students to track their progress effectively.

Students Progress Tracking

Our platform includes advanced tools for tracking student progress. Students and parents can view detailed reports of completed lessons, quiz scores, and overall performance, helping to identify areas for improvement and celebrate achievements.
